## Outreach Workflow

### Phase 1: Preparation
1. ‚úÖ Extract contact information from all projects
2. ‚úÖ Identify projects with RESEARCH_NOTES (need updates)
3. ‚úÖ Prepare customized messages for each project
4. ‚úÖ Create tracking spreadsheet for responses

### Phase 2: Initial Contact
1. **GitHub Issues**: Open issues for all 29 projects with RESEARCH_NOTES
2. **Direct Contact**: Email/Discord/Telegram for projects with contact info
3. **Social Media**: Twitter mentions for projects with handles
4. **Community**: Post in Discord/Telegram servers where accessible

### Phase 3: Follow-up
1. Track responses and contributions
2. Thank contributors publicly
3. Update research based on feedback
4. Highlight community contributions in commits

### Phase 4: Ongoing
1. Monthly reminder to projects without responses
2. Quarterly full update requests to all projects
3. Recognition program for top contributors
4. Blog posts highlighting community improvements
