Fluidkey Interface

The Fluidkey Interface allows users to seamlessly manage, receive, and send onchain assets while protecting their privacy.

Available Chains

Fluidkey is currently available on Base, Optimism, Arbitrum, Polygon, Gnosis, and Ethereum mainnet.

Available Tokens

With Fluidkey, you can receive, send, swap, and bridge ETH, USDC, and any other ERC-20 token.
