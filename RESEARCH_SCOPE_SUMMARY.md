## Research Methodology

All projects will follow our "constitutional research" methodology:
- **Multi-source verification** - Cross-reference multiple sources
- **No fabrication** - Only verified data included
- **Honest gaps** - Document what we don't know
- **Confidence scoring** - Rate data quality (0.0-1.0)
- **Community collaboration** - Invite corrections
