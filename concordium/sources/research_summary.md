## Executive Summary

Concordium is a **Layer-1 blockchain** focused on **privacy-preserving compliance** with protocol-level identity verification. It's the first blockchain to implement built-in identity verification while maintaining user privacy through Zero-Knowledge Proofs.

**Key Differentiator**: Concordium uniquely combines regulatory compliance (KYC/AML) with privacy preservation at the protocol level, protected by Swiss law.
