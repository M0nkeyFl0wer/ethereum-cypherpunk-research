Oasis ROFL

ROFL is a powerful framework that unlocks verifiable off-chain compute while utilizing on-chain guarantees like decentralized key management and liveness. By offering Intel TDX TEEs (Trusted Execution Environments), ROFL brings developers new tools for verifiable AI, trustless agents, and much more.

Oasis Sapphire

Sapphire is the first and only confidential EVM that empowers Web3 with Smart Privacy — privacy that is flexible to your requirements (100% private, `100% public, or anywhere in between).

Oasis Privacy Layer

The Oasis Privacy Layer (OPL) provides plug-and-play privacy to existing dApps on any EVM network.
