# Incognito

Incognito is a privacy protocol described as 'Incognito mode for your crypto'. The privacy protocol uses bulletproofs, ring signatures, and one-time addresses to provide privacy for blockchain transactions. Incognito allows users to prove transactions are valid without exposing sensitive information, enabling private cryptocurrency transactions.
