# Inco

Inco is the universal confidentiality layer for Web3, providing a modular Layer-1 blockchain that combines Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE), Zero-Knowledge proofs, Trusted Execution Environments (TEE), and Multi-Party Computation (MPC). Inco offers two products: Inco Lightning (powered by TEE for near-zero latency) and Inco Atlas (using FHE/MPC for maximum privacy). The network enables confidential storage, computing, and access control for Ethereum and other public blockchains, supporting use cases like private AMMs, dark pools, under-collateralized lending, and gaming with hidden information.
