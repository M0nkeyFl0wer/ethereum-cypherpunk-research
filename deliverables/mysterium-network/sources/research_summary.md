## Executive Summary

**Mysterium Network** is an active, open-source decentralized VPN (dVPN) ecosystem built on blockchain technology. Founded in 2017 in Switzerland with a development team in Lithuania, the project launched its testnet in 2018 and mainnet in 2021. The network operates as a peer-to-peer system with 20,000+ residential IPs across 135+ countries, using WireGuard protocol with ChaCha20-Poly1305 encryption.
