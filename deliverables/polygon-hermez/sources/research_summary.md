## Executive Summary

**Polygon Hermez** is a zero-knowledge rollup (ZK-rollup) scaling solution for Ethereum that was acquired by Polygon in August 2021 for **$250 million** and subsequently integrated into the Polygon zkEVM ecosystem. The project combines advanced cryptographic techniques (ZK-SNARKs) with layer 2 scaling to provide privacy-preserving, high-throughput transactions on Ethereum.

**Key Finding**: This is a MAJOR Web3 privacy infrastructure project that merged into Polygon's broader zkEVM strategy, representing one of the largest acquisitions in the zero-knowledge technology space.
