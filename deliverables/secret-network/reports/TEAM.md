### Tor Bair
**Role**: Founder, Secret Foundation (Departed)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Previous Role | Core contributor, sole shareholder of Secret Foundation Inc. |
| Status | Departed (following 2023 controversy) |

**2023 Controversy**:
In January 2023, a significant dispute arose:
- Guy Zyskind alleged an undisclosed sale of foundation tokens
- Tor Bair claimed funds were owed as part of his SCRT vesting schedule
- $2.625 million dividend payment was disclosed
- Multiple validators departed the network
- Led to calls for transparency and governance reform

**Note**: This controversy relates to governance and fund management, not the underlying technology. Secret Network's privacy guarantees were not affected.

1. **Academic Credentials**: MIT research background rare in blockchain

## Team Concerns
