# Zano

Zano is an open-source privacy-centric cryptocurrency ecosystem launched in 2019 that offers enterprise-grade privacy, security, and scalability using advanced cryptographic techniques like ring signatures (size 16) and stealth addresses. Its Zarcanum technology introduces the world's first hidden amounts Proof of Stake scheme, delivering the same privacy level as the most private Proof of Work systems. Zano enables confidential assets and user-creatable privacy tokens with complete transactional privacy.
